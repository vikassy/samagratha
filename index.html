SAMAGRATHA

Samagratha Foundation Trust (R) established on 18th November 2013 and registered. Five women from different walks of life joined their hands and initiated to form a trust to encourage women to take up entrepreneurship and develop skill in different areas to stand on their own.

Inspiration

Shri. S.T.Somashekar Gowda, MLA and philanthropist is the source of inspiration and encouraged women to form a forum to help the poor to take up income generating activities , entrepreneurship activities, empower women, develop skills etc. His motivation resulted in formation of this trust.
